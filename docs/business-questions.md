# Business Questions

This document defines the business problem this analysis addresses and the
specific questions the Power BI dashboard is designed to answer.

## Business Context

**Meridian Precision Manufacturing** is a mid-sized manufacturing facility
located in the Dallas-Fort Worth (DFW) industrial corridor, Texas, supplying
precision components to the aerospace and electronics sectors.

## Decision This Analysis Supports

The Plant Operations team has a limited preventive maintenance budget for the
next quarter. This analysis identifies which production lines, shifts, and
failure types generate the highest downtime cost, in order to prioritize
maintenance investment where it will have the greatest impact on capacity.

## Key Business Questions

1. What is the current Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE), overall and by
   production line?
2. How does OEE break down into Availability, Performance, and Quality -
   where is the largest loss concentrated?
3. What are the leading causes of downtime, and how much do they cost?
4. Are there patterns by shift (Morning / Afternoon / Night) or day of week?
5. Is OEE trending up or down over the analyzed period?
6. If the top 3 downtime causes were addressed, what capacity could
   realistically be recovered?
